# Supply Chain Transparency & ESG Compliance

## Problem

The EU Supply Chain Due Diligence Act (LkSG / CSDDD) requires companies to document and audit their entire supplier network for human rights and environmental risks. Most companies have no automated way to correlate supplier data, audit findings, and risk scores — and face significant fines for non-compliance.

MoE Codex provides a lineage-tracked supplier intelligence graph: supplier data is ingested, catalogued, and versioned, with full provenance trails for every risk assessment decision. Natural-language queries allow compliance teams to identify gaps without SQL expertise.

## Compliance Checklist

- [ ] LkSG (Lieferkettensorgfaltspflichtengesetz): risk documentation and audit trail
- [ ] EU CSDDD: due diligence evidence chain via Marquez lineage
- [ ] ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards): data trail for ESG disclosures
- [ ] No personal data in supplier risk pipeline (unless named individuals are supply chain actors)
